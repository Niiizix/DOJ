Actualités

Restez informé des dernières initiatives du Department Of Justice, des changements de politique et des activités d’application de la loi.

December 18, 2024
Law Enforcement

Major Drug Trafficking Ring Dismantled in Multi-State Operation

Federal agents coordinated with local law enforcement agencies across five states to successfully dismantle a sophisticated drug trafficking organization. The operation resulted in 47 arrests and the seizure of over $12 million in illegal substances and assets. This collaborative effort demonstrates the effectiveness of inter-agency cooperation in combating organized crime networks that threaten community safety.

December 15, 2024
Civil Rights

Department Announces Enhanced Civil Rights Enforcement Initiative

The Department of Justice has launched a comprehensive initiative to strengthen civil rights enforcement nationwide. The program includes increased funding for hate crime investigations, expanded training for federal prosecutors, and new partnerships with community organizations. This initiative reflects the Department's commitment to protecting the constitutional rights of all Americans and ensuring equal justice under the law.

December 12, 2024
Cybersecurity

FBI Cybersecurity Task Force Reports Significant Progress Against Ransomware

The Federal Bureau of Investigation's cybersecurity division announced major breakthroughs in the fight against ransomware attacks targeting critical infrastructure. New detection methods and international cooperation efforts have led to the disruption of three major ransomware operations and the recovery of millions in cryptocurrency payments. The task force continues to work with private sector partners to enhance cybersecurity defenses.

December 10, 2024
Justice Programs

New Grant Program Supports Community-Based Crime Prevention

The Office of Justice Programs has announced a new $50 million grant program designed to support community-based crime prevention initiatives. The program will provide funding to local organizations, community groups, and law enforcement agencies to develop innovative approaches to reducing crime and improving public safety. Applications will be accepted through March 2025, with awards expected to be announced in late spring.

December 8, 2024
Policy

Updated Federal Sentencing Guidelines Released

The Department has published updated federal sentencing guidelines that reflect recent legislative changes and judicial precedents. The revised guidelines emphasize rehabilitation programs and alternative sentencing options for non-violent offenders while maintaining strong penalties for serious crimes. Legal professionals and criminal justice stakeholders are encouraged to review the comprehensive documentation available on the Department's website.

December 5, 2024
Training

National Law Enforcement Training Academy Expands Programs

The National Law Enforcement Training Academy has announced the expansion of its specialized training programs to include advanced courses in digital forensics, community policing, and crisis intervention. These new programs reflect the evolving challenges faced by law enforcement professionals and provide cutting-edge training methodologies. The expanded curriculum will be available to federal, state, and local law enforcement personnel starting in early 2025.
