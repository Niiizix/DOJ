Join the Department of Justice

The Department of Justice is actively recruiting dedicated professionals to serve in various capacities across our agencies. We are committed to building a diverse workforce of highly qualified individuals who share our mission of upholding justice and protecting the American people.

Whether you're interested in law enforcement, legal services, cybersecurity, administration, or specialized investigations, the DOJ offers challenging and rewarding career opportunities that make a real difference in communities nationwide.

Eligibility Requirements

To be considered for employment with the Department of Justice, candidates must meet the following minimum requirements:

  • U.S. citizenship is required for all positions
  • Must be at least 21 years of age (23 for certain law enforcement positions)
  • Bachelor's degree from an accredited institution (minimum requirement for most positions)
  • Clean criminal background with no felony convictions
  • Ability to obtain and maintain appropriate security clearance
  • Physical fitness standards must be met for law enforcement roles
  • Successful completion of polygraph examination where required
  • Drug screening and ongoing random testing compliance
  • Valid driver's license with acceptable driving record
  • Willingness to relocate based on agency needs
  • Strong written and verbal communication skills
  • Demonstrated integrity and ethical decision-making
