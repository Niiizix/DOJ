Welcome to the Department of Justice

The United States Department of Justice is committed to upholding the rule of law, keeping our country safe, and protecting civil rights. We work tirelessly to ensure justice and fairness for all Americans.

Our mission is to enforce federal laws, provide federal leadership in preventing and controlling crime, and ensure fair and impartial administration of justice for all Americans.
